Content Ideas — Campaign Series
Idea 01
The "You've Been Scammed" Storytime
Relatable story format — open with a common scam scenario (Instagram vendor, freelancer ghosting, car deal gone wrong). End with how PayHold would have prevented it.
"She sent ₦150k. He blocked her. This is what escrow is for."
Idea 02
Vendor vs. Buyer Trust Series
Two-sided perspective posts — one day post from a buyer's fear ("What if I send and they don't deliver?"), next day from a seller ("What if they don't pay after I ship?"). PayHold solves both.
"Both sides win. That's the deal."
Idea 03
Use Case Carousel
Slide-by-slide carousel showing all the deals you can protect: freelance work, car purchases, real estate deposits, vendor payments, event bookings, Jiji/WhatsApp sales.
"If money changes hands — PayHold should be in the middle."
Idea 04
"Build in Public" Founder Posts
Behind-the-scenes content about building PayHold — why it was created, the problem you experienced personally, the tech stack, milestones. Builds trust and authenticity.
"I built PayHold because I got burned too."
Idea 05
Myth-Busting Reels / Posts
Short punchy posts busting myths: "Escrow is only for big deals" / "It's complicated" / "It costs too much" — followed by the truth about PayHold's simplicity.
"Myth: Escrow is for corporates only. Truth: ₦5k deal? Still protected."
Idea 06
Testimonial / Social Proof
Quote-style posts from early users or beta testers. Clean card design with a quote about the confidence PayHold gave them on a deal. Strong trust signal for new visitors.
"I finally felt safe buying from a stranger online."
Idea 07
Before/After Comparison
Split-screen posts: left side shows a stressful unsafe deal, right side shows the same deal done through PayHold. Visual simplicity, strong contrast storytelling.
"Same deal. Different outcome. Escrow changes everything."
Idea 08
Weekly "Deal of the Week"
Highlight a type of real-world deal that could be protected each week (this week: car deposits; next week: freelance design projects). Keeps content fresh and specific.
"This week we're protecting your car deposit. 🚗"
Idea 09
Interactive Poll / Question Posts
Engagement-first posts: "Have you ever been scammed buying online? 🙋‍♂️" / "What's the biggest deal you've done without protection?" — drives comments and visibility.
